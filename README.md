# Irfan Emre Utkan

Software developer building **FORCE**, a strength training app for workout logging, recovery tracking, and coaching.

## FORCE

[FORCE - Training Log & Coach](https://apps.apple.com/tr/app/force-training-log-coach/id6757546311) is live on the App Store.

The app includes workout tracking, exercise history, recovery insights, training analytics, and AI-assisted coaching.

## Tech
